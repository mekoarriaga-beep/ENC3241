# Instructions

How to Tie Your Shoe

1) Place your foot inside the shoe.
 
2) Pull both ends of laces upward.
 
3) Hold left lace in left hand.
 
4) Hold right lace in right hand.
 
5) Cross the right lace over the left lace.
 
6) Pull the right lace under the left lace.
 
7) Pull both laces outward.
 
8) Make a loop with the left lace.
 
9) Hold the loop with your left hand.
 
10) Make a loop with the right lace.
 
11) Hold the loop with your right hand.

12) Cross the right loop over the left loop.

13) Push the top of the right loop through the opening between loops.

14) Pull right loop outward.

15) Pull left loop outward.

16) Pull on loops till adequately tight.
